# Lens: growth at a reasonable price and knowing what you own — v1

This lens distills public Peter Lynch writing. Its signature exit reversal is to challenge both holding losers too long and selling intact winners too early.

## Five principles

1. Own businesses you can explain simply.
2. Match growth with a reasonable price rather than paying any price for growth.
3. Look for long growth runways and hold long enough for compounding to matter.
4. Remove broken stories and keep watering intact winners.
5. Avoid excessive leverage and complexity; do the research yourself.
